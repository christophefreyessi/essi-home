About Us



The European System-Driven Semiconductor Initiative (ESSI) is a European think-tank.
It aims at identifying and promoting practical actions to increase and accelerate the generation of economic value between European semiconductor product makers and system makers.
The ESSI vision is to enable the fastest deployment of differentiated market-leading systems, leveraging the best European semiconductor products. ESSI aligns with and fuels the European ChipAct
